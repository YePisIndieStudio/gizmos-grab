Who We Are?


We are a dynamic team of five passionate individuals who came together to create GizmosGrab, a tech gadget selling website designed for seamless user experience and efficient functionality.

Sanchit Mishra serves as our Live Programmer and also contributes to backend development, ensuring smooth functionality and real-time updates on the platform.

Paripurna Mishra is the Project Manager, skillfully overseeing timelines, coordination, and the successful execution of all project milestones.

Tanishq Pillai handled the creation of our presentation materials (PPTs), helping translate our technical efforts into a clear, impactful narrative.

Abhishek Jha is our creative UX Designer, focusing on crafting an intuitive, user-friendly interface that enhances the customer journey.

Prateek Hembade leads our Testing and Bug Reporting, ensuring that GizmosGrab runs smoothly, is free of errors, and delivers a reliable experience to users.

Together, we combined our unique skills to build a project that reflects both technical excellence and user-centric design.
